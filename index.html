I am a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST Graduate School of AI, working with Prof. Sungsoo Ahn. I received my Ph.D. in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering from KAIST in 2026, advised by Prof. Jihan Kim, where my dissertation focused on closing the simulation-experiment gap in metal-organic frameworks (MOFs).

My research interests include first-principles modeling (DFT) and machine learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs), automated discovery and data-driven design of materials, and generative models for materials.
