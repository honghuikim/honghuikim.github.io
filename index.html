I am a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST Graduate School of AI, working with Prof. Sungsoo Ahn. I completed my Ph.D. in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at KAIST, advised by Prof. Jihan Kim.

I work on AI for materials science, aiming to bridge the gap between computation and experiments. My goal is to enable real-world materials discovery with AI.
